Invest in Impact. Earn with Purpose.

Quarm Investments, LLC provides a unique opportunity to invest in real estate that delivers strong returns while improving the lives of real people in real communities. Our model is built on integrity, transparency, and community value.

Investment Philosophy

We focus on Iowa’s undervalued residential market — acquiring distressed or vacant homes, renovating them to modern standards, and offering them as high-quality affordable housing. Our approach not only improves neighborhoods, but also generates healthy returns for our investors.

Projected Return Framework

  • Projected ROI: 18–25% per project
  • Break-even expected within 12–18 months
  • Short-term equity with long-term reinvestment strategy

Capital Use Breakdown

  • 45% Acquisition: Direct purchase of property
  • 25% Rehab & Construction: Licensed contractors, energy-efficient upgrades
  • 15% Operating Reserves: Covers taxes, insurance, vacancy cushion
  • 10% Community Initiatives: Workshops, internships, nonprofit partnerships
  • 5% Admin: Legal, compliance, accounting

How You’re Protected

  • Projects backed by physical collateral (real estate)
  • Quarterly reporting and financial summaries
  • Clear project timelines and expense tracking
  • Diversification into multiple units and neighborhoods

2025–2027 Strategy

  • 2025: Launch flagship redevelopment (8–10 units)
  • 2026: Stabilize and lease-up; evaluate impact metrics
  • 2027: Launch two additional properties across Iowa
  • 2028–2030: Expand into 3–5 multi-site projects per year
