Flipping Real Estate. Building Value.

Quarm Investments, LLC is a bold, minority-led real estate investment company dedicated to transforming Iowa neighborhoods through strategic housing redevelopment. Founded in 2025, we combine hands-on expertise in construction, finance, and property management to breathe new life into underutilized properties.

Our Mission

To revitalize underserved neighborhoods through strategic real estate investment — creating high-quality housing, empowering underrepresented communities, and delivering long-term value to our investors and the families we serve.

What We Stand For

  • Community Revitalization: We reinvest in neighborhoods others have overlooked.
  • Smart Growth: We use data-driven acquisition and renovation strategies to generate returns.
  • Hands-On Execution: Our team manages every phase from acquisition to leasing.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: We are 33.33% minority-owned and advocate for equitable development.
  • Long-Term Sustainability: Our projects are built to benefit both people and place, for years to come.

We don’t just flip houses — we elevate families, renew streets, and build the kind of wealth and stability our communities deserve. Explore our work. Join our mission. Invest in change.
